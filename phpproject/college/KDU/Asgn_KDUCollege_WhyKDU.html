Why KDU

    KDU Penang University College is a subsidiary of Paramount Education, the education arm of Paramount Corporation Bhd (PCB) and is a fully-integrated education services provider, offering national and international curriculum primary and secondary schools through to undergraduate and post-graduate as well as executive and professional development programmes. The group includes the reputable Sri KDUÂ® School and KDU University College, a pioneer in Pre-U and twinning programmes with campuses in Penang, Damansara Jaya and Petaling Jaya.

    Paramount Education has had 80,000 students since its inception in 1983, testament to its 33-year track record for providing good quality, high value education, anchored on the promise of shaping characters and building careers. Paramount Education is listed on the Main Market of Bursa Malaysia Securities Berhad and whose diversified business interests also include property development through its subsidiary, Paramount Property. Being an affordable college in Malaysia, our focus is on building skillsets through our comprehensive academic programmes that are all accredited by the Malaysian Qualifications Agency.

    Our state-of-the-art modern facilities provide a conducive environment for students to expand their knowledge and enhance their skills in-line with modern real word requirements. As recognition towards our contribution, KDU Penang was awarded the prestigious 6-star College status by the Ministry of Higher Education MyQuest Audit 2014/2015. Our consistency in excellence allowed us to become the best university college in Penang, Malaysia. KDU Penang has always been an affordable college in Malaysia as we aspire to make quality education more accessible. Placing character building and growth in our curriculum, we have enjoyed an impressive track record throughout the years.

    Our students have successfully won numerous national and international competitions. Alongside an impressive graduate employment ratio record, each KDU student is shown to be well-prepared for the real world. Over the years, Paramount Education may have grown in size and strength but its commitment remains the same: To provide quality education, be it in academics or life in general. KDU believes that people should learn more from people and be inspired by their life's experiences. A value-driven approach in building careers and enriching our students have made us the best university college in Penang, Malaysia.
