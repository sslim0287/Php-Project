Student Life @ SEGi

    Student Experience
    You will experience an education that prepares you for success, exposes you to new ideas and equips you with skills and knowledge to excel in your chosen career.
    Student Clubs & Societies Sports & Recreation AEC Leadership Programme
    SEGi has over 100 student clubs, societies and collectives to choose from. We provide a platform for students to lead a healthy and positive lifestyle. Provide opportunities for students to practice their leadership skills.

    We aim to do everything we can to support you during your journey and beyond. A variety of support services are available to students including learning support, career advice, financial assistance and disability support.
