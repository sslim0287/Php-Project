    Overview

    Since 1983, KDU has been leading the way for private higher education in Malaysia. As a real world university meeting real world needs, students at KDU are nurtured to be competent graduates who hit the ground running and are able to adapt seamlessly into the real world, no matter what programme they choose to delve into.
